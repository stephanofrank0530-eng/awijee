Welcome to AWIJEE Tours and Safaris

We are a small and passionate Tanzanian tour company offering affordable safari adventures, beach holidays, mountain climbing, and cultural experiences across the country.

Our Vision

To become a trusted local tourism partner that showcases Tanzania’s natural beauty, culture, and wildlife to the world through responsible and memorable travel experiences.

Our Mission

To provide safe, affordable, and well-organized tours that exceed guest expectations while supporting local communities and protecting the environment.

Our Services

Wildlife Safaris

Affordable game drives in national parks and reserves with experienced local guides.

Mountain Climbing

Mount Kilimanjaro trekking support including guides, planning, and logistics.

Beach Holidays

Relaxing beach tours in Zanzibar, Dar es Salaam, and Tanga.

Cultural & City Tours

Explore traditions, markets, history, and daily life with local experts.

Tourist Transport

Reliable transport for safaris, city tours, and airport transfers.

Custom Tour Planning

Flexible tour packages designed around your budget and schedule.
